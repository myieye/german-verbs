<font color="#FFCE00"><strong>Weak [regular] Verbs:</strong></font><br>
Verb stem never changes. Past Participle ends with '-t'. Simple Past ends with '-te'. Verb endings are completely regular.<br>
Present tense endings: ich:'-e', du:'-st', er/sie/es:'-t', wir:'-en', ihr:'-t', sie/Sie:'-en'.
<br><br>
<font color="#FFCE00"><strong>Strong [irregular] Verbs:</strong></font><br>
Verb stem sometimes changes. Past Participle ends with '-en'. Verb endings are irregular.
<br><br>
<font color="#FFCE00"><strong>Mixed Verbs: (a.k.a. Weak irregular Verbs)</strong></font><br>
Verb stem sometimes changes. Past Participle ends with '-t'. Simple Past ends with '-te'. Verb endings are irregular.
<br><br>
<font color="#FFCE00"><strong>Modal Verbs: (also Mixed Verbs)</strong></font><br>
[mögen, dürfen, können, müssen, sollen, wollen, möchten]<br>
Past Participle changes to infinitive when used with another infinitive (except 'mögen') (eg. 'ich habe es machen müssen')
